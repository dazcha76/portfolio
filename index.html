About Me

Hi! I'm Danika and I'm a Front End Developer. I fell in love with programming back in college, when I started creating websites to share information with friends and family without having to clog their emails.


Once I decided I wanted to make a career out of it, I started taking online classes and enrolled in bootcamps. With every new language, framework, and library I learned, I became more impressed with the things I could accomplish.


The best thing about becoming a programmer is that things are always changing and I'll never run out of things to learn!
